# RouteTrust Component Inventory

- Button: primary, secondary, destructive
- Card: base surface
- MetricCard: KPI-first card
- StatusBadge: exact state mapping only
- AlertBanner: operational alerts and blockers
- Timeline: route and incident chronology
- RouteStatus: route summary strip
- DriverStatus: driver availability and route ownership
- IncidentCard: severity, owner, impact, next step
- ApprovalPanel: human approval flow
- EmptyState: explain absence and next action
- LoadingState: explicit skeleton or loading copy
- ErrorState: plain failure explanation and impact
- HelpTooltip: short, contextual, operational
- LanguageToggle: public bilingual switch
