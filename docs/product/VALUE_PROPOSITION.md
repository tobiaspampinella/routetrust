# Value Proposition

RouteTrust gives logistics teams a visual operating layer that is easier to trust than raw spreadsheets, fragmented chats, or opaque trackers.

## For Customers

- clear tracking status
- visible ETA
- immediate delay visibility
- simple next-step messaging
- low visual noise

## For Business Managers

- route and driver visibility
- operational KPIs in one view
- incident and SLA risk awareness
- demo and CMS control for product validation
- honest system-status surfaces

## Why It Sells

- faster operational understanding
- lower ambiguity during delivery exceptions
- stronger product demo narrative
- better executive visibility without pretending full enterprise completion
