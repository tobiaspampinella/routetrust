# CEO Audit

Generated: 2026-06-01
Role: CEO Strategy Agent

## Verdict

RouteTrust has a credible product direction and a strong demo nucleus. It is not yet a company-ready SaaS product.

## Strategic Reality

- Vision is coherent: AI-built, human-orchestrated operational intelligence for logistics.
- Current product is still a local web tester/demo.
- Market promise is larger than backend reality.
- The biggest CEO risk is selling implementation before operational truth exists.

## Focus

Do not expand features. Build the minimum real SaaS spine:

1. tenant
2. users/roles
3. routes/stops/drivers
4. delivery events
5. approvals
6. audit logs
7. customer tracking
8. reports

## Strategic Risks

- False readiness claims.
- Losing trust with first pilot due to demo persistence.
- Overinvesting in AI/agents before DB and workflow.
- No ICP-specific packaging.

## Decision

Pursue controlled beta with 1 design partner only after staging and DB-backed core flows exist.
