## Operational Fit

Strong:

- route progress demo

Weak:

- no operating day lifecycle

- no dispatch closeout

2. Build routes.
3. Assign drivers.
4. Start dispatch.
5. Monitor risk.
6. Resolve exceptions.
7. Approve critical changes.
8. Close operating day.
9. Export report.
10. Review audit log.
