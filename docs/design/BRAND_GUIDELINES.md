# Brand Guidelines

## Brand Idea

RouteTrust is an operational intelligence product. The tone must feel controlled, visual, and dependable.

## Keep

- direct language
- operational nouns
- honest product status
- human oversight framing

## Avoid

- futuristic hype
- autonomy theater
- decorative dashboard noise
- consumer-app whimsy

## Visual Direction

- dark graphite foundation
- strong blue operational accent
- restrained use of green, amber, and red
- high-contrast metric surfaces
- minimal ornamental gradients
